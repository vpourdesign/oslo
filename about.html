Since the 1990s

A Story of
Earned Growth

Since its founding in the 1990s, Oslo Construction has steadily grown into one of the most important companies in industrial mechanics in Quebec. Our growth is not the result of chance — it is the fruit of meticulous, deliberate work by a team of devoted managers who, day after day, earned the trust of their clients.

So much so that today, our clients choose us to bid on every future project that requires our expertise. Over recent years, Oslo Construction has stood out by working regularly with Canada's largest companies.

Three decades of industrial excellence in the heart of Quebec.

1990

The Beginning

Oslo Construction is founded in Terrebonne, Quebec, with a clear mission: deliver industrial mechanical work with uncompromising craftsmanship. A small but determined team lays the foundation of what will become one of the province's most trusted names in heavy industry.

2000

Building Partnerships

A decade in, our reputation speaks for itself. Oslo earns the trust of Quebec's most demanding industrial clients — securing recurring contracts with leaders like Pratt & Whitney, ArcelorMittal, Kruger, Bombardier and Rio Tinto Alcan. Our proactive management philosophy becomes our calling card.

TODAY

A Name They Call First

Today, our clients don't just keep us on their roster — they choose Oslo to bid on every future project that demands our expertise. SNC-Lavalin, Hydro-Québec, and Canada's top industrial companies choose us not because we're convenient, but because we deliver.

Our Philosophy

Proactive
Management

We apply our "proactive" management philosophy to all of our projects — whether they involve plant shutdowns, maintenance work, or long-term capital projects. To ensure optimal management of every project, Oslo Construction adheres to three principles represented by the pyramid.

At the base of all our concerns: Health, Safety & Environment. The body of the pyramid represents the budget we work within and must respect — bounded by quality control on one side and schedule compliance on the other.

Flexible Engagements

Three Ways
to Work
With Us

To meet your specific needs, we offer the flexibility to manage contracts on a lump-sum, time & material, or cost-plus basis.

01
📋

Lump Sum

A fixed price for a defined scope of work. Maximum cost certainty for you — maximum accountability for us. Ideal for well-defined projects with clear specifications.

02
⏱️

Time & Material

Billed on actual labour hours and materials used. Best suited for maintenance work, shutdowns, and projects where scope evolves. Full transparency at every step.

03
📊

Cost-Plus

Reimbursement of all project costs plus a fixed margin. An open-book approach for complex, long-duration projects where collaboration and trust are paramount.
